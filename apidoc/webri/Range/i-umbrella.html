Range#umbrella
umbrella(r)

From /home/trans/Documents/programs/current/facets/doc/ri/Range/cdesc-Range.yaml

Returns a two element array of the relationship between two Ranges.

Diagram:

    Relationship     Returns

  self |-----------|
  r    |-----------|    [0,0]

  self |-----------|
  r     |---------|     [-1,-1]

  self  |---------|
  r    |-----------|    [1,1]

  self |-----------|
  r     |----------|    [-1,0]

  self |-----------|
  r     |-----------|   [-1,1]

  etc.
Example:

  (0..3).umbrella(1..2)  #=&gt;  [-1,-1]
