Float#round_to
round_to( n )

From /home/trans/Documents/programs/current/facets/doc/ri/Float/cdesc-Float.yaml

Rounds to the nearest _n_th degree.

  4.555.round_to(1)     #=&gt; 5.0
  4.555.round_to(0.1)   #=&gt; 4.6
  4.555.round_to(0.01)  #=&gt; 4.56
  4.555.round_to(0)     #=&gt; 4.555
