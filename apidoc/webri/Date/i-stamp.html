Date#stamp
stamp(format=:default)

From /home/trans/Documents/programs/current/facets/doc/ri/Date/cdesc-Date.yaml

Convert to a formatted string. See DATE_FORMATS for predefined formats.

This method is aliased to to_s.

Examples:

  date = Date.new(2007, 11, 10)       # =&gt; Sat, 10 Nov 2007

  date.stamp(:db)            # =&gt; &quot;2007-11-10&quot;
  date.stamp(:short)         # =&gt; &quot;10 Nov&quot;
  date.stamp(:long)          # =&gt; &quot;November 10, 2007&quot;
  date.stamp(:rfc822)        # =&gt; &quot;10 Nov 2007&quot;

Adding your own formats to stamp

You can add your own formats to the Date::FORMAT hash. Use the format name as the hash key and a strftime string as the value. Eg.

  Date::FORMAT[:month_and_year] = &quot;%B %Y&quot;
