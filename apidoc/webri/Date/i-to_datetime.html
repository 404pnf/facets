Date#to_datetime
to_datetime()

From /home/trans/Documents/programs/current/facets/doc/ri/Date/cdesc-Date.yaml

Converts a Date instance to a DateTime, where the time is set to the beginning of the day and UTC offset is set to 0.

Example:

  date = Date.new(2007, 11, 10)  # =&gt; Sat, 10 Nov 2007

  date.to_datetime               # =&gt; Sat, 10 Nov 2007 00:00:00 0000
