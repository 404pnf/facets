OpenStruct#instance_delegate
instance_delegate()

From /home/trans/Documents/programs/current/facets/doc/ri/OpenStruct/cdesc-OpenStruct.yaml

Provides access to an OpenStruct's inner table.

  o = OpenStruct.new
  o.a = 1
  o.b = 2
  o.instance_delegate.each { |k, v| puts &quot;#{k} #{v}&quot; }
produces

  a 1
  b 2

(also known as ostruct_delegate)
