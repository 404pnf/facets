Date#to_time
to_time(form=:local)

From /home/trans/Documents/programs/current/facets/doc/ri/Date/cdesc-Date.yaml

Converts a Date instance to a Time, where the time is set to the beginning of the day. The timezone can be either :local or :utc (default :local).

  date = Date.new(2007, 11, 10)  # =&gt; Sat, 10 Nov 2007

  date.to_time                   # =&gt; Sat Nov 10 00:00:00 0800 2007
  date.to_time(:local)           # =&gt; Sat Nov 10 00:00:00 0800 2007

  date.to_time(:utc)             # =&gt; Sat Nov 10 00:00:00 UTC 2007
