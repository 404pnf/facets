Array#rotate
rotate(n=1)

From /home/trans/Documents/programs/current/facets/doc/ri/Array/cdesc-Array.yaml

Rotates an array's elements from back to front n times.

  [1,2,3].rotate      #=&gt; [3,1,2]
  [3,1,2].rotate      #=&gt; [2,3,1]
  [3,1,2].rotate      #=&gt; [1,2,3]
  [1,2,3].rotate(3)   #=&gt; [1,2,3]
A negative parameter reverses the order from front to back.

  [1,2,3].rotate(-1)  #=&gt; [2,3,1]
