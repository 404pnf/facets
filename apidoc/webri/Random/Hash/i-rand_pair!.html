Random::Hash#rand_pair!
rand_pair!()

From /home/trans/Documents/programs/current/facets/doc/ri/Random/Hash/cdesc-Hash.yaml

Deletes a random key-value pair and returns that pair.

  a = {:one =&gt; 1, :two =&gt; 2, :three =&gt; 3}
  a.rand_pair!  #=&gt; [:two, 2]
  a             #=&gt; {:one =&gt; 1, :three =&gt; 3}

(also known as pick_pair)
