Array#combination
combination(k=2) {|s.values_at(*idx)| ...}

From /home/trans/Documents/programs/current/facets/doc/ri/Array/cdesc-Array.yaml

Yields the block to each unique combination of n elements.

  a = %w|a b c d|
  a.combination(3)
produces

  [[&quot;a&quot;, &quot;b&quot;, &quot;c&quot;],
   [&quot;a&quot;, &quot;b&quot;, &quot;d&quot;],
   [&quot;a&quot;, &quot;c&quot;, &quot;d&quot;],
   [&quot;b&quot;, &quot;c&quot;, &quot;d&quot;]]
