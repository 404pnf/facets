Class#cattr_accessor
cattr_accessor(*syms)

From /home/trans/Documents/programs/current/facets/doc/ri/Class/cdesc-Class.yaml

Creates a class-variable attr_accessor that can be accessed both on an instance and class level.

  class MyClass
    cattr_accessor :a
  end

  MyClass.a = 10
  MyClass.a           #=&gt; 10
  mc = MyClass.new
  mc.a                #=&gt; 10
