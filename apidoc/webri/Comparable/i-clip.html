Comparable#clip
clip(lower, upper=nil)

From /home/trans/Documents/programs/current/facets/doc/ri/Comparable/cdesc-Comparable.yaml

Returns self if above the given lower bound, or within the given lower and upper bounds, otherwise returns the the bound of which the value falls outside.

  4.clip(3)    #=&gt; 4
  4.clip(5)    #=&gt; 5
  4.clip(2,7)  #=&gt; 4
  9.clip(2,7)  #=&gt; 7
  1.clip(2,7)  #=&gt; 2
