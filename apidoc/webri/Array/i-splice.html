Array#splice
splice(*args)

From /home/trans/Documents/programs/current/facets/doc/ri/Array/cdesc-Array.yaml

Splice acts a combination of #slice! and #store. If two arguments are given it calls #store. If a single argument is give it calls slice!.

  a = [1,2,3]
  a.splice(1)    #=&gt; 2
  a              #=&gt; [1,3]

  a = [1,2,3]
  a.splice(1,4)  #=&gt; 4
  a              #=&gt;[1,4,3]
