cmp(other)

Compare method that takes length into account. Unlike #<=>, this is compatible with #succ.

  &quot;abc&quot;.cmp(&quot;abc&quot;)   #=&gt;  0
  &quot;abcd&quot;.cmp(&quot;abc&quot;)  #=&gt;  1
  &quot;abc&quot;.cmp(&quot;abcd&quot;)  #=&gt; -1
  &quot;xyz&quot;.cmp(&quot;abc&quot;)   #=&gt;  1
