String#underscore
underscore()

From /home/trans/Documents/programs/current/facets/doc/ri/String/cdesc-String.yaml

The reverse of camelcase. Makes an underscored of a camelcase string.

Changes '::' to '/' to convert namespaces to paths.

Examples

  &quot;SnakeCase&quot;.underscore           #=&gt; &quot;snake_case&quot;
  &quot;Snake-Case&quot;.underscore          #=&gt; &quot;snake_case&quot;
  &quot;SnakeCase::Errors&quot;.underscore   #=&gt; &quot;snake_case/errors&quot;
