The reverse of camelcase. Makes an underscored of a camelcase string.

Changes '::' to '/' to convert namespaces to paths.

Examples

  &quot;SnakeCase&quot;.snakecase           #=&gt; &quot;snake_case&quot;
  &quot;Snake-Case&quot;.snakecase          #=&gt; &quot;snake_case&quot;
  &quot;SnakeCase::Errors&quot;.snakecase   #=&gt; &quot;snake_case/errors&quot;
