Class#cattr_reader
cattr_reader( *syms )

From /home/trans/Documents/programs/current/facets/doc/ri/Class/cdesc-Class.yaml

Creates a class-variable attr_reader that can be accessed both on an instance and class level.

  class MyClass
    @@a = 10
    cattr_reader :a
  end

  MyClass.a           #=&gt; 10
  MyClass.new.a       #=&gt; 10
