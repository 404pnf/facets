MatchData#matchtree
matchtree(index=0)

An alternate to #to_a which returns the matches in order corresponding with the regular expression.

  md = /(bb)(cc(dd))(ee)/.match &quot;XXaabbccddeeffXX&quot;
  md.to_a       #=&gt; [&quot;XXaabbccddeeffXX&quot;, &quot;bb&quot;, &quot;ccdd&quot;, &quot;dd&quot;, &quot;ee&quot;]
  md.matchtree  #=&gt; [[&quot;bb&quot;], [&quot;cc&quot;, [&quot;dd&quot;]], &quot;ee&quot;]
